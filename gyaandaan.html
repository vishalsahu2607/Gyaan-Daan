Gyaan Daan

Join as an education volunteer to support underprivileged children in nearby government schools.

Our mission

Gyaan Daan is a volunteer initiative for people who want to contribute their free time to teach children from disadvantaged backgrounds. Our goal is to bring quality tutoring and mentoring to students in the nearest government schools.

How you can help

  • Offer tuition in basic subjects like math, science, and language.
  • Help students build confidence and study skills.
  • Support the growth of children who need extra guidance.

If you have a few hours each week, you can make a positive difference in a child's life.
